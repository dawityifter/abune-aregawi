# Abune Aregawi Church App - Comprehensive Demo Script (15 Minutes)

**Goal**: Demonstrate the end-to-end capabilities of the platform, highlighting specific roles (Guest, Member, Relationship, Secretary, Treasurer, Admin) and deep-diving into unique features like Twilio Voicemail and Permanent Meeting/Task tracking.

---

## 1. Introduction & Guest Experience (0:00 - 1:30)
**Role**: **Guest / Public**
**Visual**: Home Page (Unauthenticated)

**Narration**:
"Welcome to the comprehensive demonstration of the Debre Tsehay Abune Aregawi Tigray Orthodox Church management platform. This application is not just a website; it’s a complete digital ecosystem designed to connect our community and streamline our church’s operations.

Let’s start with the EXPERIENCE for a **Guest**—someone visiting us for the first time.

The platform is built to be accessible to everyone. As you can see on the Home Page, we provide all essential information—Service Times, Location, and our Mission—up front.

**Interactive Features**:
*   **Live Stream**: If there is a prayer service or spiritual teaching happening right now, the **YouTube Live** video automatically appears right here on the main page, so you never miss a moment of worship.
*   **Calendar**: We also feature an up-to-date **Interactive Calendar** that displays the **7 Major Fasts** and **9 Major Feasts** of our church year, keeping our faithful connected to the liturgical season.

Crucially, we serve a bilingual community. Watch as I toggle the language switch in the top corner. Instantly, the entire interface—menus, content, and buttons—transforms from English to Tigrigna ('Tigrigna' view). This isn't just a Google Translate overlay; these are dedicated, culturally accurate translations ensuring every member feels at home ('English' view back)."

---

## 2. The Member Experience (1:30 - 4:00)
**Role**: **Member**
**Visual**: Registration -> Dashboard -> Profile

**Narration**:
"Now, let’s log in as a **Member**.
Registration is simplified to encourage adoption--members can sign up using securely using their **Phone Number**. We strictly use phone numbers to ensure every account is tied to a real person and to facilitate our SMS communication features.

Once logged in, I arrive at the **Member Dashboard**. This is the command center for our congregation. Rather than a cluttered menu, we use these intuitive 'Cards' to guide users.

**Smart Customization**:
It is important to note that **this dashboard is smart**. Not everyone sees the same cards. The system automatically shows you only the tools relevant to your specific role—whether you are **Church Leadership**, a **Secretary**, or in the **Relationship Department**.

**Highlight Features**:
1.  **Profiles & Family**: I can manage my own profile, but more importantly, our **Head of Household** feature allows me to link and manage my spouse and children’s profiles, ensuring the church has accurate records for the whole family.
2.  **Member Dues**: Transparency is key. I can click here to see exactly where I stand with my monthly dues or pledges.
3.  **Digital Gallery**: Access to our community’s history. From baptisms to festivals, members can browse high-quality images in the gallery—now fully localized in Tigrigna as well.

This self-service model empowers members and reduces the administrative burden on our staff."

---

## 3. The Relationship Department (4:00 - 6:30)
**Role**: **Relationship / Outreach Team**
**Visual**: Outreach Dashboard

**Narration**:
"Moving to our internal teams, let’s look at the **Relationship Department**. Their mission is connection—welcoming new faces and maintaining bonds with our partners.

I’m switching to a user with the 'Relationship' role. Notice the dashboard changes: I now have access to the **Outreach Dashboard**.

**Deep Dive**:
*   **Onboarding**: The system automatically flags new registrations. My team can see a list of 'New Members' who haven't been welcomed yet. We can mark them as 'Contacted' and record notes on their integration.
*   **External Partners**: We also track our relationships with other churches and relief organizations here. It’s a dedicated CRM right inside our church app, ensuring no partner or new member falls through the cracks."

---

## 4. Secretary: Meetings & Permanent Decisions (6:30 - 9:30)
**Role**: **Secretary / Church Leadership**
**Visual**: Department Dashboard -> Meeting Details

**Narration**:
"Now, let’s look at one of our most powerful features: **Meeting & Task Management**, designed for the **Secretary** and **Church Leadership**.

Churches run on meetings, but often decisions are lost in emails or paper notebooks. Our system changes that.

**Action: Create a Meeting**
I’ll navigate to the 'Administration' department and create a new meeting.
All fields are **Bilingual by Design**: I can enter the Agenda and Minutes in both English and Tigrigna side-by-side. This ensures that minutes sent to the congregation are accurate in both languages.

**Action: Task Creation & Tracking**
During the meeting, we make decisions. Instead of just writing them down, I create a **Task** right here in the meeting record.
*   *Title*: 'Prepare Harvest Festival Program'
*   *Assignee*: I can assign this directly to a specific member.
*   *Priority*: High.

**The Power of Memory**:
When we convene for our *next* meeting, the system automatically pulls forward 'Tasks from Previous Meeting'. We can see exactly what is Pending, In Progress, or Completed.
We are building a **Permanent Record** of decisions. Years from now, leadership can look back and see not just *what* was decided, but *who* was assigned to it and *when* it was completed."

---

## 5. Treasurer: Financial Stewardship (9:30 - 12:30)
**Role**: **Treasurer**
**Visual**: Treasurer Dashboard

**Narration**:
"Financial integrity is paramount. Let’s switch to the **Treasurer** role.
The **Treasurer Dashboard** is a secure, restricted environment for managing the church's resources.

**Deep Dive Features**:
1.  **Overview Stats**: Real-time visualization of Total Collections vs. Expenses. The 'Collection Rate' metric helps us understand member commitment trends.
2.  **Bank Integration**: This is a game-changer. We support CSV uploads from major banks like Chase. The system ingests the bank statement and helps the Treasurer match transactions to specific members.
3.  **Member Lookup**: A Treasurer can instantly pull up any member to answer questions like 'Did my Zelle payment go through?' or 'Am I behind on dues?'.
4.  **Expense Management**: We don't just track income. Expenses are logged with categories and receipts, allowing us to generate a Net Income report at any moment."

---

## 6. Admin & Communications: Twilio Integration (12:30 - 14:30)
**Role**: **Admin / Super Admin**
**Visual**: Admin Dashboard -> Voicemail Inbox / SMS Broadcast

**Narration**:
"Finally, the **Admin** role facilitates the technical and communication backbone of the church.

We have integrated **Twilio** to give the church a dedicated voice. This isn't just about sending texts; it's a two-way communication channel.

**Deep Dive: Voicemail Inbox**
Members can call our dedicated church phone number and leave a message.
*   In the **Voicemail Inbox**, leadership can press 'Play' to listen to these messages securely within the app.
*   We even provide an automated **Transcription**, so you can read the message at a glance.
*   Once handled, we archive it. This ensures every community concern is heard and addressed.

**SMS Broadcast**:
Need to announce a schedule change? The Admin can send a bulk SMS to all 'Active Members' in seconds. It allows us to cut through the noise of social media and reach our people directly on their phones."

Thank you."
